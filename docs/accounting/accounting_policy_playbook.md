# Accounting Policy Playbook

## Prepaids

Prepaid expenses should be recorded as assets when cash is paid before the related service period is consumed. Expense recognition should occur over the coverage period using a documented schedule approved during month-end review.

## Accruals

Accrual entries should be used when an obligation has been incurred before the vendor invoice or cash payment is received. Every accrual should identify the source support, expected reversal timing, and owner responsible for clearing the balance.

## Revenue Recognition

Patient service revenue should be recognized from approved production and collections workflows only. When collections timing differs from service timing, finance should review whether the entry represents earned revenue, deferred revenue, or a receivable-driven recognition event.

## Review Requirement

All AI-drafted accounting guidance is advisory only. A designated reviewer must confirm the final treatment against approved internal accounting policy before the entry is posted or communicated as authoritative guidance.
