# Month-End Close Checklist

## Journal Draft Review

Every drafted journal entry must be reviewed for balanced debits and credits, valid account codes, open accounting period status, and the presence of supporting business context before approval.

## Reconciliation Expectations

Balance sheet accounts should be reconciled to source support each close cycle. Any unreconciled difference should be documented with owner, cause, and remediation date.

## Policy Citation Expectations

When HAL provides accounting guidance, the reviewer should verify that citations come from approved local policy documents or documented operating procedures. Guidance without supporting citations should be treated as low confidence and escalated.

## Approval Control

AI-generated accounting outputs are drafts. They may inform the close process, but they do not replace controller or owner approval for final entries, policy decisions, or external reporting.
